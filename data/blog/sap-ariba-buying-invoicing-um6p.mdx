## Context

This case study is based on a SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing implementation delivered for UM6P in an education and IT environment, with integration to SAP S/4HANA MM and SAP Ariba DSN.

## Problem

The objective was to support a procurement operating model that could connect requesters, procurement teams, suppliers, goods receipt activity, and invoice processing in a more structured and traceable way.

## My role

I supported implementation and customization activities across workshops, test scenarios, migration follow-up, issue resolution, train-the-trainer preparation, and key-user enablement. My role was shared across business and delivery coordination rather than positioned as sole ownership.

## System landscape

- SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing
- SAP Ariba DSN / Business Network
- SAP S/4HANA MM

## Approach

I contributed to:

- workshops with procurement, requester, and finance stakeholders
- scenario preparation for invoice, ASN, and receipt-to-invoice flows
- follow-up on migration and issue resolution during readiness phases
- key-user support and train-the-trainer enablement materials

The delivery emphasis was on keeping the process understandable for end users while preserving integration consistency with SAP S/4HANA MM.

## Outcome

The implementation reached deployment readiness with structured support for supplier enablement, invoice and ASN processing, and stable receipt-to-invoice operational flows.

## What this demonstrates

This work demonstrates practical P2P delivery capability across SAP Ariba, SAP S/4HANA MM integration, testing, issue follow-up, and user enablement in a live business setting.
