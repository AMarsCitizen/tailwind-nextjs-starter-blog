## Context

This sanitized case study is based on a customs compliance and procurement automation initiative built around supplier invoices retrieved through SAP Ariba DSN and connected operational references.

## Problem

Customs and procurement teams needed faster visibility on SH/HS codes and import-related compliance signals. Manual invoice review was time-consuming and made it harder to anticipate customs requirements early enough in the process.

## My role

I contributed as a SAP Ariba consultant and automation contributor. I supported the OCR/AI approach, developed Python processing logic, and helped structure the output so it could be reused by compliance and customs-clearance users.

## System landscape

- OCR & AI processing
- Python
- SAP Ariba DSN
- SAP S/4HANA
- ADiL customs references
- DMP / ONSSA reference checks

## Approach

The workflow combined document extraction, code identification, and validation logic:

- supplier invoices were collected from the SAP Ariba DSN flow
- OCR and AI logic extracted candidate SH/HS information
- Python processing prepared standardized outputs
- customs references were used to cross-check codes and improve compliance traceability

## Outcome

In this context, the solution reduced manual invoice analysis by an estimated 60%, improved anticipation of customs requirements, and accelerated preparation for customs-clearance follow-up.

## What this demonstrates

This work demonstrates how I connect SAP procurement flows with automation logic, regulatory controls, and structured operational outputs rather than treating AI as a disconnected experiment.
