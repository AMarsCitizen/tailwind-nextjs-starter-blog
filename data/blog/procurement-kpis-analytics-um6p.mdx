## Context

This case study is based on procurement analytics work delivered for UM6P to improve reporting quality and operational visibility across SAP Ariba and SAP S/4HANA data.

## Problem

Procurement stakeholders needed more than static reporting. They needed a structured KPI layer that could support CAPEX/OPEX monitoring, spend visibility, and recurring management follow-up without manual consolidation every cycle.

## My role

I extracted and modelled procurement data, supported KPI-definition workshops, and contributed to the documentation needed for operational reporting. The work sat between business reporting needs and technical data structuring.

## System landscape

- Power BI
- SAP S/4HANA OData
- SAP Ariba APIs
- Procurement KPI and spend-monitoring datasets

## Approach

The delivery was organized around:

- identifying the core procurement indicators needed by business stakeholders
- structuring a data model that could combine SAP Ariba and SAP S/4HANA sources
- defining calculations, reporting views, and documentation for reuse
- aligning the reporting output with day-to-day procurement monitoring needs

## Outcome

The result was a reporting foundation covering 30+ procurement KPIs for CAPEX/OPEX and spend monitoring, helping teams move from static visibility toward more proactive procurement management.

## What this demonstrates

This work demonstrates my ability to translate procurement questions into data structures, KPI logic, reporting models, and adoption support that business teams can actually use.
